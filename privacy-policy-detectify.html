Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use AI Detector: Detectify.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, images, or documents processed through our app. The application only collects minimal, non-personal technical data such as crash reports and performance metrics to ensure stability and improve functionality.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Processing

Our service uses third-party APIs to perform analysis:

All uploaded content is transmitted securely and directly to these providers for analysis. We do not intercept, store, or reuse this content.

While we do not store your content, we ensure secure HTTPS communication between the application and third-party services. Our partners maintain technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
