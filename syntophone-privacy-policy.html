Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Syntophone ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Syntophone mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements in the App. AdMob may collect and use device information, advertising identifiers, and usage data to serve personalized ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase may collect usage data, device information, and crash reports. This data is used solely to improve app performance and user experience. For more information, please review Google Firebase's Privacy Policy at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect purchase-related information and anonymous identifiers. For more information, please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

How We Use Information

The information collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze app usage and improve user experience
  • Process in-app purchases
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability

Data Storage and Security

We do not store any personal data on our servers. All data collected by third-party services is stored and managed by those respective services in accordance with their privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Disable analytics tracking by limiting ad tracking on your device
  • Request information about data collected by third-party services by contacting them directly

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
