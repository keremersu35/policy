Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 6, 2025

1. Introduction

LoFi Guru (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our application, which generates lofi music through Fal.ai (including the cassetteai/music-generator model). We want to make it very clear: LoFi Guru does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not ask for or process any personally identifiable information (such as name, email, phone number, or account details). You can use LoFi Guru without creating an account, without logging in, and without providing any personal data.

3. Technical Data

The only data handled by the app is non-identifiable, technical information used solely for performance monitoring:

  • Firebase Crashlytics: Used to detect crashes and improve stability. Data may include generic device information (e.g., model, OS version) and crash reports. This information cannot be used to identify individual users.
  • Firebase Analytics (optional): Used to track general usage patterns (e.g., feature usage, session length). These are aggregated and anonymous statistics only.

4. Third-Party Services

LoFi Guru uses third-party services to deliver AI-generated music:

  • Fal.ai: Provides the AI infrastructure. LoFi Guru only sends music generation prompts; Fal.ai does not receive or store personal information from our users. See Fal.ai Privacy Policy.
  • CassetteAI: A model hosted on Fal.ai for music creation. It does not directly collect personal data from LoFi Guru users. See CassetteAI Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase: Used exclusively for anonymous crash and performance monitoring. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent personal data — because none is collected in the first place. Technical data may be processed by Firebase or Fal.ai solely for the purposes of operating the services.

6. Children’s Privacy

LoFi Guru is safe for all audiences. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no risk of misuse of children’s data. Our app complies with applicable child privacy regulations (such as COPPA).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If significant changes are made, we will provide notice through the app. Continued use after changes means acceptance of the updated policy.
