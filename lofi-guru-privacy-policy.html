Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 6, 2025

1. Introduction

LoFi Guru (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application, which generates lofi music using Fal.ai (including the cassetteai/music-generator model). We also use Firebase to monitor performance, not to collect personal data for marketing or profiling.

2. Information We Collect

LoFi Guru itself does not collect or store personal information from users. However, limited technical data may be automatically collected for performance and stability monitoring via Firebase, such as:

  • Crash logs and diagnostic data (e.g., device type, OS version, error messages)
  • App usage statistics (e.g., session duration, feature interactions)

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates third-party services. Their data practices are governed by their own policies:

  • Fal.ai — Provides the AI music generation service. According to Fal.ai Privacy Policy, they may collect information such as name, email, phone number (if provided), IP address, device identifiers, and analytics data through cookies or similar technologies.
  • CassetteAI (Pixl Technologies, Inc.) — Powers the music generation model. As outlined in the CassetteAI Privacy Policy, they may collect identifiers (name, email, device ID), crash data (IP, OS version), and analytics information. CassetteAI also complies with child privacy laws and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Used strictly for app analytics and crash reporting. See Firebase Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Data

Data collected by third-party services is primarily used to:

  • Monitor and improve application performance
  • Debug technical issues and prevent crashes
  • Ensure stable and secure functionality of AI-generated music

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data.

5. Data Sharing

LoFi Guru does not share personal information directly. However, technical and analytical data may be processed by Fal.ai, CassetteAI, and Firebase as part of their service delivery. Please review their individual privacy policies for detailed information.

6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Request access to the data collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict or opt out of analytics collection

To exercise these rights, please contact us or the relevant third-party provider directly.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If such data is discovered, we will ensure its deletion as required by applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be communicated via the app or our website. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance.
