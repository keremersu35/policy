Privacy Policy for 2000s Camera

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

Simple Summary: We don't collect your photos or personal information. We only collect anonymous analytics and performance data to improve the app.

1. What We Collect

We DO NOT collect:

  • Your photos or images
  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Your location data
  • Any personal or identifiable information

We DO collect:

  • Analytics Data: Anonymous app usage statistics (which features you use, how often you open the app)
  • Performance Data: Crash reports and technical issues to fix bugs
  • Device Information: Device model and iOS version (to ensure compatibility)
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status to unlock premium features

2. How We Use This Information

  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To understand which features are most popular
  • To manage your subscription and unlock Pro features
  • To make the app better for everyone

3. Your Photos

  • All photos you take are stored only on your device
  • We never upload, access, or see your photos
  • Your photos never leave your phone unless you choose to save them to your photo library or share them

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services to make the app work:

Firebase Analytics (by Google)

  • Collects anonymous usage data and crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

RevenueCat

  • Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases
  • Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Apple App Store

  • Processes payments (Apple handles all payment info, not us)
  • Privacy Policy: apple.com/legal/privacy

5. Permissions

Camera Access:

  • Required to take photos with vintage filters
  • We never upload or store these photos

Photo Library Access (Optional):

  • Only needed if you want to save photos to your device
  • You can deny this permission and still use the app

6. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted when transmitted to our analytics services
  • We don't store any personal data on our servers
  • Your photos stay on your device - we never see them

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information at all, the app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Major changes will be announced in the app.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to delete or access. However, you can:

  • Delete the app to stop all data collection
  • Manage permissions in your iOS Settings
  • Cancel your subscription anytime through the App Store
