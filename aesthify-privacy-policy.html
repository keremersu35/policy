Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Welcome to Aesthify. We value your privacy and are committed to being transparent about the limited data we collect. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information (PII). You can use the app without creating a traditional account.

The only data we process includes:

  • Generated Content: The text prompts you enter and the images generated by the AI.
  • Anonymous User ID: A random, unique identifier assigned to your installation to manage your credits and gallery locally and in the cloud.
  • Device Integrity Data: Technical signals (via Apple App Attest) to verify that requests are coming from a legitimate version of our app, preventing bot abuse.
  • Crash & Performance Logs: Anonymous technical data (e.g., device model, OS version, crash traces) to help us fix bugs and improve app stability.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services solely for infrastructure and stability purposes:

Firebase (Google)

Purpose: Backend infrastructure.

  • Authentication: Uses anonymous identifiers to secure your access.
  • Firestore & Storage: Stores your generated images and prompts so you can access them in your gallery.
  • App Check: Verifies app integrity to prevent abuse.
  • Crashlytics: Collects anonymous crash reports.

AI Providers

Purpose: Image generation.

We send your text prompts and image parameters to third-party AI models to generate the requested images. These providers process this data solely to fulfill your request.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited data collected strictly for:

  • Generating and displaying the images you request.
  • Maintaining your credit balance and gallery history.
  • Ensuring the security and stability of the app.

We do NOT use your data for marketing, personalization, or tracking across other apps and websites.

4. Data Retention

Your generated images and prompts are stored in our secure cloud database associated with your anonymous ID. If you delete the app or clear your data, you may lose access to this history as we have no way to link it back to you personally.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
