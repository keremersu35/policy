Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WallDone ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our iOS application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

WallDone stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Tasks: Your to-do items, including task names, completion status, priority levels, categories, and due times
  • Custom Themes: Your custom wallpaper themes, including colors, layouts, positions, and background images you choose
  • App Preferences: Your app theme preference (light/dark/system) and selected wallpaper theme

Analytics and Crash Data

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve app performance and user experience. This includes:

  • App usage statistics and feature engagement
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Anonymous user identifiers

Subscription Information

When you make a purchase, payment processing and subscription management is handled by RevenueCat and Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not store or have access to your payment card information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide core app functionality (task management and wallpaper generation)
  • Save your preferences and custom themes
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs through analytics and crash reports
  • Manage your subscription status

3. Data Storage and Security

All your personal data (tasks, themes, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is:

  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Protected by your device's security features
  • Backed up through iCloud if you have iCloud backup enabled (controlled by your iOS settings)
  • Completely deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For analytics and crash reporting
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and payment processing
  • Apple In-App Purchases: For processing payments

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

WallDone does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain content specifically directed at children.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your data (all data is stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in iOS Settings)
  • Opt out of analytics (through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: sup.detectify@gmail.com

9. Data We Do NOT Collect

To be clear, we do NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your task content or personal to-do items
  • Your custom wallpaper images or designs
  • Your location data
  • Your contacts or photos (beyond wallpaper backgrounds you explicitly choose)
  • Your browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Any personally identifiable information beyond what's required for analytics and subscriptions

This privacy policy is effective as of the date stated above and applies to all users of WallDone.
